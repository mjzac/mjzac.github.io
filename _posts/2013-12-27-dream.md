"Listen to your heart, follow your dream." I have been thinking about this sentence for last few days. What's so special about dream? 

1. **Your dream is all yours.** You are the architect (term courtesy: [*Inception*](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1375666)). It means there is no limit in what you dream. No one is there to stop you from doing anything!

2. **Creativity.** Unleash the creativity in you by dreaming high. You can dream about all the fantasies in the world (walking through some serene place or owning a Lamborgini. Its up to you :P)

3. **Passion.** Dreams can make one passionate. Just dreaming is good for nothing unless one is passionate about it.



But how does one listen to his/her heart? Well, it means no one knows you better than you do. If you want to do something, just **do** it! Now here comes the list specifying why you shouldn't do it. Most people are defeated by the fear of failure about achieving their dream. But the saddest part is that they doesn't even try before giving up. **Dreams are made to be followed**.

Great minds of the world are great dreamers. Isaac Newton, Albert Einstein, Steve Jobs are few examples. They have changed the world in their own way. The world praises them for their achievements. What they did was they just followed their dreams.

But why should one follow his/her dream? The answer is too simple. For your [**happyness**](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0454921/). It's true you have to work very hard to fulfil your dream. You will have to face many obstacles. You may get demotivated due to failures. But still you are living your dreams, not someone else's, and one day you will make your dream come true.
